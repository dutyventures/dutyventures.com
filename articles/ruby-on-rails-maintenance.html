The Hidden Problems in Your Ruby on Rails Application

You might not think much about your app’s performance until something breaks. But waiting until there's a critical issue can cost your business much more in downtime, lost customers, and even data breaches. Here are some issues that are probably already affecting your web application:

1. Performance Issues Are Killing Your User Experience

Studies show that even a 1-second delay in page load time can lead to a 7% reduction in conversions. Your slow-loading application might be costing you revenue without you even realizing it.

2. Outdated Code and Security Vulnerabilities

Failing to regularly update your application can expose your business to:

  • Security breaches, including data theft and hacking
  • Compliance issues with regulations such as GDPR
  • Loss of trust from your customers if their data is compromised

3. You’re Accumulating Technical Debt

Without ongoing refactoring and cleanup, your application can become harder to update, scale, or troubleshoot, leading to more expensive fixes in the future.
